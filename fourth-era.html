2015 - 2020

These Hidden Gems are comic books and graphic novels from between 2015 and 2020


Basketful of Heads

Author: Joe Hill
Artist: Leomacs
Publishers: DC Comics
Description: June Branch is in trouble. She's trapped on Brody Island with nowhere to run. Her boyfriend, Liam, has been kidnapped. And four bloodthirsty escaped convicts will stop at nothing to find her. All poor June has to defend herself with is a strange Viking axe with the terrifying power to decapitate a person and leave their head still talking. If she's going to save Liam and herself, June will have to keep a cool head...or even a whole basketful of them!

Home Sick Pilots

Author: Dan Watters
Artist: Caspar Wijngaard
Publishers: Image Comics
Description: In the summer of 1994, a haunted house walks across California. Inside is Ami, lead singer of a high school punk band—who's been missing for weeks. How did she get there? What do these ghosts want? And does this mean the band has to break up?.

Doctor Who: The Eighth Doctor

Author: George Mann
Artist: Emma Vieceli
Publishers: Titan Comics
Description: From a sleepy Welsh village under attack from living art, to symbiotic shards on a crystalline planet, from a theater haunted by the Silvered, to a stately home overrun by the ancient woods, and on to the great Resurrection Barges of the Bakti – the Eighth Doctor and new companion Josie Day are on course for a grand adventure like no other!

Something is Killing the Children

Author: James Tynion IV
Artist: Werther-DellEdera
Publishers: BOOM! Studios
Description: When the children of Archer's Peak—a sleepy town in the heart of America—begin to go missing, everything seems hopeless. Most children never return, but the ones that do have terrible stories—impossible details of terrifying creatures that live in the shadows. Their only hope of finding and eliminating the threat is the arrival of a mysterious stranger, one who believes the children and claims to be the only one who sees what they can see.

Shade, the Changing Girl

Author: Cecil Castellucci
Artist: Marley Zarcone
Publishers: DC Comics
Description: Loma Shade may be from another planet, but she’s still like every other twenty something who feels that their life is going nowhere fast. Bored out of her mind, she steals a "madness coat" and astrally projecting herself across space, Loma ends up in the body of Megan Boyer, an Earth girl who seems to have it all: youth, beauty and a conveniently damaged brain. Following her “miraculous” recovery, however, Loma finds there’s just one problem with being Megan: Everyone hates her. She was a bully who terrorized her enemies and her friends alike, and now Loma’s stuck with the consequences.

Odoru Respawn

Author: Kentarou Mikashima
Artist: Kentarou Mikashima
Publishers: Young Magazine the 3rd
Description: Like an RPG character, Choushi Dannoura possesses a constitution that allows him to respawn at the last place he slept whenever he dies. When he saves An Yamazaki's life, she falls madly in love with him. Will he able to respond in tune with her heavy love? The curtain opens on a sinister comedy.

Massacre Happy End

Author: Arata Miyatsuki
Artist: Hirokazu Mukoura
Publishers: Manga Park
Description: High school student Kusakabe Makoto has to work all day to raise money for the treatment of his younger sister, who suffers from a serious illness. The father is unemployed, the mother ran away, leaving the children, bullying at work ... Despite all this, he does not lose hope and continues to live for her sister. But having learned that she did not have long, Makoto falls into despair. Fear of the unknown causes him to take a different look at the value of human life.

Stone Star

Author: Jim Zub
Artist: Max Dunbar
Publishers: Swords and Sassery
Description: The nomadic space station called Stone Star brings gladiatorial entertainment to ports across the galaxy. Inside this gargantuan vessel of tournaments and temptations, foragers and fighters struggle to survive. A young thief named Dail discovers a dark secret in the depths of Stone Star and has to decide where his destiny lies - staying hidden in the shadows or standing tall in the searing spotlight of the arena. Either way, his life, and the cosmos itself, will never be the same!
